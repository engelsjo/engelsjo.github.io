About

I obtained a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Michigan State University in April, 2021. My research is in the area of fingerprint recognition. Within fingerprint recognition, I have worked on a number of problems including, spoof detection, representation learning, encrypted matching, infant recognition, fingerphoto matching, system evaluations, and synthesis. Prior to studying at Michigan State, I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Grand Valley State University with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science. I plan to join the Amazon One team in June, 2021
